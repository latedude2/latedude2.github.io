Resource Management game made during Inclusivity Game Jam 2021

Developed in 48 hours together with 4 of my friends. For this project, I acted as lead programmer.

Technologies used: Unity, C#, Backtrace.

Links:
  • Play the game on itch.io
  • Github Repository

The themes of the Game Jam was "Inclusivity" and "Find it, fix it". The goal of the game is to find a way to distribute the tasks to different pirates who have unique traits and keep the ship afloat.

I worked on designing and programming the game systems, controlling the characters and implemented the water shader.
