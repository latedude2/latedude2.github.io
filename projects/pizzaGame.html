Bloody Bitty Caesars

Nordic Game Jam 2023 submission. Together with 4 of my friends, we created a creepy driving game where the player has to drive around town delivering pizzas.

Technologies used: Unity, C#, GameCI.

Links:
  • Check the game out on Itch.
  • Github Repository

Drive for your life as you deliver pizzas to quirky and pizza-desperate customers. Do you think you can deliver all the pizzas?

I implemented the driving physics, the camera controller, random generation of goals, the navigational aid and the post-processing in URP.
