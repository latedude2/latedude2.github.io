Rhythm game made during GMTK Game Jam 2021

Developed in 48 hours together with 4 of my friends. For this project, I acted as the team lead and programmer.

Technologies used: Unity, C#.

Links:
  • Play the game on itch.io
  • Github Repository

The theme of the Game Jam was "Joined together". We decided to develop a rhythm game where a group of friends join together in a circle to dance.

I worked on programming the behavior of the dancers and obstacles on the dance floor while the other programmer in the team focused on the rhythm part of the game.

I also setup the post-processing effects for the game. Adding bloom, chromatic aberration and hue shift really made the game come together.
