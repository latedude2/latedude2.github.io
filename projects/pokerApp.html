Poker App

A university project developed by me and 5 other students during at Aalborg university. For this project, I acted as the team lead and programmer. We developed an app which connected to a Python server which analysed images taken by a camera. The app then told the players what poker hand they have and how strong it is.

Technologies used: Android, Python, OpenCV.

During this course, we were encouraged to develop our own algorithms for analysing images. This image shows an algorithm I developed which used the spread of the closest pixels to the center to determine the suit of the cards.

BLOB analysis was used to determine the number of the card.

The UI showing what poker hand the user has. The player's personal cards are entered manually by the user each time he has a new hand.
