Language Learning Narrative Research

A university project developed by me and 6 other students during at Aalborg university. For this project, I acted as lead programmer. We developed an app which taught Danish in collaboration with a Danish language school IA Sprog. A lot of user testing was done to made sure the difficulty of the game matched the target group. The goal of the research was to see if narrative had an impact on intrinsic motivation of students when learning a new language.

Technologies used: Android, UML.
