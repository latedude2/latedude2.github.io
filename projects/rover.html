Space exploration/survival game made during Game Jam round 2 by Backtrace

Developed in 48 hours together with 4 of my friends. For this project, I acted as lead programmer.

Technologies used: Unity, C#, Backtrace.

  • Gameplay video

The theme of the Game Jam was "Sharing knowledge". As a captain on a mission to find a habitable planet, you send off a rover to planet Idrade. You control it from your space shuttle orbiting around the planet. Your aim is to find a source of water with the assistance of the rover. You must send commands for the rover to scan the area for water and direct it to a potential water source.

I worked on implementing the command line interface and game logic for the enemies and combat. As the game focuses on slow transmission of information, I implemented delays in the command sending and receiving. Also, the information of enemy positions are updated with delay.
