EggMission

Global Game Jam Kaunas 2017 submission. Together with 3 of my friends, we created a small arcade driving game themed around the Easter holiday.

Links:
  • Trailer
  • Download and Play - Windows build

The goal of the game is to avoid police while illegally buying and selling chocolate eggs with toys inside them.

I edited the whole trailer in half an hour. And that will be clear when you see the trailer, but I had my fun with it :D The original plan was to record my own Honda Civic drifting, but we decided against it to save on time :(
